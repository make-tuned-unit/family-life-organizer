The idea behind Kinrows

A family does better when it rows together.

Kin, pulling in the same direction — that's the whole name. When everyone can see what needs doing, takes an oar, and cheers the others on, a household stops feeling like a juggling act and starts feeling like a team.

Share the load

So much of family life is invisible — the remembering, the planning, the "did anyone…?". Research on the household mental load is blunt: when that work is made visible and shared, stress falls and partnership grows. Kinrows brings it into the open, so no one carries it alone.

Pull in the same direction

Children who know their family's story — its plans, its rhythms, its in-jokes — are measurably more resilient and secure. One shared space for the calendar, the trips and the everyday keeps everyone in the same boat, not five separate ones.

Celebrate every win

Decades of relationship research land on the same finding: a win that's shared and cheered is worth far more than one kept quiet. Kinrows surfaces the milestones — a first goal, a finished challenge, a small victory — so the whole family gets to celebrate together.

Private by design

Your family's life stays your family's.

Kinrows is built around households. Your home's calendar, money, lists and messages are walled off to the people you've actually invited — enforced at the data layer, not just hidden in the interface.

We don't sell data and we don't run ads. There's nothing to mine, because the app isn't built to mine it.

Household isolation

Each home's data is scoped to its members. Other families can't see it — by design, in the database.

No ads, no data sale

The app is free because it's simple and honest — not because you're the product.

You choose who's in

Invite people you trust. Share across families with clans only when you want to.

Optional add-on · Concierge

A quiet hand, only if you want one.

The app is complete on its own. Concierge is an optional helper that knows your whole household — calendar, lists, budget, trips, birthdays — keeps you ahead of it, and, when you ask, quietly runs the app for you. Turn it on if it helps. Leave it off and nothing changes.

A morning brief

One warm, prioritised read on your family right now — what's today, what's slipping, what needs a decision.

Runs the app for you

Ask in plain words — "add Leo's recital Friday at 6," "move the dentist to Thursday," "start a packing list for the cabin," "we settled on the blue couch." It creates, edits and clears things across your calendar, lists, decisions, trips and more — then tells you exactly what it did.

Gentle nudges

"Trip in 14 days, no stay booked yet." "Leo's birthday is close." Helpful, throttled, never naggy.

Remembers — and you stay in control

It keeps your family's context, so you can pick a conversation back up days later instead of starting over. Quick summaries run on-device, you choose what it can read, and you can clear what it remembers anytime.

Receipts & meal ideas

Snap a receipt and it logs the spend for you; ask what's for dinner and get real ideas from a few ingredients. The features that lean on AI live here.

$9.99 / month, per household — one seat unlocks Concierge for everyone at home. It's the home for everything that uses AI to keep your family on track — the daily brief, managing the app by just asking, proactive nudges, receipt scanning and meal ideas. Always optional; the core app is, and stays, free.
